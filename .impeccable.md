## Design Context

### Users
Students and self-learners turning PDFs (lecture notes, chapters, cheatsheets) into spaced-repetition flashcards. They use Mnemo in focused study sessions — often late evenings, often tired, often returning to a half-forgotten deck. The emotional job is: *make reviewing feel less like a chore and more like a small daily ritual they want to return to.*

### Brand Personality
**Playful & encouraging.** Duolingo-adjacent, not Duolingo-loud. Streaks are celebrated, copy has warmth and dry wit, correct answers feel satisfying, frustrating moments (missed cards, errors) are softened with empathy. Three words: **warm, encouraging, earned-fun.** The product rewards returning, not logging in.

### Aesthetic Direction
Current codebase is monochromatic (pure-gray oklch, zero chroma), Raleway sans across the board, Tailwind v4 + shadcn defaults, rounded cards, clean type hierarchy. The delight pass should NOT burn this down — instead add warmth inside the existing monochrome frame: tinted neutrals, a single accent hue for reward/streak moments, purposeful motion (spring-based flips, staggered reveals, confetti for milestones), and copy that earns a smile without fishing for one. Theme: **light primary, dark supported** (both are in place, study sessions commonly happen at night).

### Design Principles
1. **Earned celebrations.** Small wins get small feedback. Big moments (session complete, streak milestones, first-of-kind) get the full treatment. Never celebrate the same thing twice.
2. **Soften failure, sharpen success.** Missed cards get empathy ("no worries, we'll bring it back tomorrow"). Correct cards get a crisp, confident beat.
3. **Motion with purpose.** Cards flip, numbers tick up, progress fills. No decoration for decoration's sake. Respect `prefers-reduced-motion`.
4. **Copy with personality, not comedy.** Warm, specific, product-aware. Never generic AI-slop loading lines ("teaching robots to dance"). Write what's actually happening.
5. **Keyboard-first reverence.** Power users study with fingers on 1–4 and Space. Hover and hidden niceties are a bonus, never the main path.
